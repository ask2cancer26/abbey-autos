What we do

Everything Your Car Needs

From a routine MOT to a full engine rebuild — all done in-house, with the same standards we've held since 1968.

01
MOT Testing
Class 4 MOT for cars, vans and taxis. Fast turnaround. Honest advice on any failures — no upselling.
02
Car Servicing
Oil changes, filters, belts and spark plugs. Interim to major service schedules. All makes and models.
03
Body Shop Repairs
Accident repairs, dents, scratches and resprays. Insurance jobs handled. Over five decades of bodywork experience.
04
Air Conditioning
Regas, leak testing and full A/C system repairs. Back to factory performance.
05
Engine & Gearbox
Full engine rebuilds, gearbox overhauls, clutch replacements. Complex mechanical work is our bread and butter.
06
Brakes & Suspension

Tyres & Exhausts

Our story

Serving SE London Since 1968

Every job carries our warranty. Every price is fair. And when we say the car's ready, it's ready — done right, not just done fast.
